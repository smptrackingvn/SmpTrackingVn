1. Introduction

This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Smallpin Logistics Android app. By using the app, you agree to the practices below, subject to your jurisdiction’s requirements and any final legal review before publication.

2. Data and on-device permissions

The app may request or use the following permissions, depending on the features you use:

  • Camera: scanning QR or barcodes and capturing photos for logistics operations (e.g. documents, cargo), initiated by you.
  • Location (precise, approximate, background): route tracking, dispatch, and location-related services when you grant permission.
  • Notifications: order status, system messages, or operational alerts.
  • Storage / photos: attaching or saving files for business use when you choose to.
  • Microphone: only if a feature requires it (e.g. voice notes); otherwise the permission may not be used.
  • Network (Internet): syncing with servers, maps, and cloud services.
  • Background services / background location: may be used for shipment tracking when the app is configured and you allow it.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for unrelated marketing purposes.

3. Service providers and SDKs

The app may integrate third-party services (e.g. cloud, crash analytics, messaging, maps) to operate the product. Data shared with those providers is limited to what is reasonably necessary. Keep your disclosures consistent with the Data safety form in Google Play.

4. Retention and security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information. Retention depends on data type and legal obligations; data no longer needed for the stated purposes is deleted or anonymized according to internal procedures.

5. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing; withdraw consent where applicable; and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Use the contact email at the top of this page to submit requests.
